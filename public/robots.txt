# emdashcms.org — crawler policy
#
# Open to every crawler. Explicit per-agent allows for the AI search
# engines are redundant with `User-agent: *` today, but they carry
# intent: we WANT ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google Gemini, and
# Microsoft Copilot to surface marketplace plugins and themes in AI
# citations. If any of these bots flips to a default-deny posture, the
# explicit entry below keeps us on the allowlist without a site change.
#
# The Content-Signals directive below keeps its original posture:
# search/AI retrieval-augmented citations are allowed, but the content
# must not be used for AI training. Plugin and theme authors retain
# their own rights via their individual repositories.

User-agent: *
Allow: /

# Content Signals (https://contentsignals.org/) — marketplace owner's position
# on AI usage. Plugin and theme authors retain their own rights via their repos.
Content-Signal: ai-train=no, search=yes, ai-input=yes
